make the ultimate journey to the akron marathon

The Akron Marathon Race Series presented by Summa Health was designed to encourage participation for all fitness levels. With distances ranging from 1-mile to the marathon, pick the series combination that suits your goals. Running the series will be easy as you follow a typical training calendar to benchmark your way to our marquee September event!

Race Series Runner Premiums

In addition to all the runner premiums you receive from the individual races, runners participating in all three events of the Akron Marathon Race Series presented by Summa Health will:

• Earn a Special Race Series Medal after completing all three events

national interstate 8k & 1 mile

Kick off the Akron Marathon Race Series presented by Summa Health with the National Interstate 1 Mile on Friday night! The 8k will be held on Saturday. Consider going for the challenge and run both events for a commemorative pint glass! The course highlights The University of Akron’s campus with a unique tour of downtown Akron.

firstenergy akron marathon, half marathon & team relay
